01 — About

About Me

I'm a passionate technology enthusiast and student entrepreneur from the Malabar region of Kerala. I love exploring gadgets, AI tools, productivity software, and emerging technologies — and turning that curiosity into something useful for others. Tech From Malabar is my way of sharing what I discover, learn, and recommend with honesty and care.

02 — Journey

My Journey

A genuine story — built through curiosity, not credentials.

The Beginning

Curiosity over credentials

I'm not a professional software engineer. I'm a student who fell in love with technology and decided to build something real — one search query, one tutorial, one bug fix at a time.

Learning by Building

Documentation, YouTube & Google

I built Tech From Malabar through continuous learning — official documentation, YouTube tutorials, Google Search, and a lot of trial and error. Every error message became a lesson.

AI as a Co-Pilot

AI-assisted development

I leaned on AI tools to learn faster, debug smarter, and ship cleaner code — but I reviewed, tested and refined every single output myself. AI accelerated my learning; it didn't replace it.

Production Ready

Countless hours, real results

Hours of testing, fixing bugs, optimizing for SEO and refining the experience turned a side project into a production-ready platform. Every feature taught me something new.
